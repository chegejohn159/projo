HOW IT STARTED

It all started with a passion for singing and playing guitar, Martin Hurtado, vocalist and lead of Derrame Norteño,has created a band playing a blend of regional Mexican music from Northern Mexico. Derrame Norteño is a beloved band in Delta, UT and has been performing for almost ten years.
